Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

We offer multiple shipping options to suit your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - Free on orders over $50
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days - $9.99
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day - $19.99

All orders placed before 2 PM EST are processed the same day. Delivery times may vary based on your location.

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase:

  • 30-Day Return Policy: Return unused and unopened products within 30 days
  • Original Packaging: Items must be in their original packaging with proof of purchase
  • Free Returns: Return shipping is free for defective products
  • Refund Processing: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide:

  • International Standard: 7-14 business days - Rates vary by location
  • International Express: 3-7 business days - $24.99
  • Customs & Duties: Additional fees may apply depending on your country's regulations

During checkout, you'll see the exact shipping costs for your location.

Tracking your order is easy:

  • Email Notification: You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships
  • Account Dashboard: Log into your account to view order status and tracking
  • Customer Service: Contact our team for real-time updates
  • SMS Updates: Opt-in for text message updates on major shipping milestones

We're committed to ethical and sustainable practices:

  • Cruelty-Free: All our products are certified cruelty-free
  • Vegan Options: Over 80% of our products are vegan-friendly
  • Natural Ingredients: We prioritize natural, sustainably sourced ingredients
  • Eco-Friendly Packaging: Our packaging is recyclable and biodegradable

Look for the vegan and cruelty-free badges on product pages.
